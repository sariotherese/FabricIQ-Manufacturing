# Business Challenge

SAP ERP — production orders, inventory, finance
MES (Manufacturing Execution) — line throughput, downtime, quality lab results
Farmer/Supplier system — coconut intake, quality grading, farmer payments
Sales & Distribution (CRM + EDI) — orders, shipments, channel performance
Sustainability tracker — water usage, emissions, organic certification data
IoT sensors — temperature, brix, pH on processing lines

# Pain points

Plant managers can't quickly answer "Why did yesterday's coconut water yield drop?"
Procurement teams lack visibility into farmer supply risk vs demand forecast
Sustainability reporting takes 3 weeks of manual Excel work each quarter
Sales leaders rely on weekly static reports — no self-service insight

# Use case
A Fabric Data Agent that any Betrimex employee can chat with to answer questions like:

"Which farmers delivered Grade A coconuts above 95% last month, and what was the average brix?"
"Show me yield loss on Line 2 yesterday and the top 3 root causes"
"What's our projected coconut shortage vs Q3 demand for the EU market?"
"Compare carbon intensity per liter for our coconut water vs coconut milk YTD"
